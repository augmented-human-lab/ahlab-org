Hopu·2023

Hopu is a mobile game that encourages children (8-12 years) to explore basic science concepts in a fun and engaging way. Phenomena in their environment, such as sound and noise levels, can be measured and recorded through discovering and hatching imaginary creatures. By carrying out their own observations, recording their methods, and collecting new creatures, we aim to motivate children and excite them to learn science.
