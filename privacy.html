Privacy Policy

Effective 3 May 2026.

1. Who we are

This site, ahlab.org, is operated by the Augmented Human Lab (AHL), a research lab studying human–computer interaction.

For the purposes of issuing digital membership credentials (Google Wallet and Apple Wallet passes), the lab is supported by an authorised issuance partner acting as the legal data controller of record:

  • FLOWXR PTE. LTD (Singapore UEN 202555502N), registered at 2 Venture Drive, #19-18, Vision Exchange, Singapore 608526, operating on behalf of Augmented Human Lab.

All references to "we", "us", or "the lab" below cover both Augmented Human Lab and its issuance partner in this capacity.

2. What we collect

We collect the minimum information needed to run the site and the membership programme.

Site visitors (everyone)

  • Standard server access logs (IP address, user agent, timestamps) retained for security and abuse prevention.
  • Anonymised usage analytics via Google Analytics 4. Used in aggregate to understand how the site is used. We do not combine analytics data with any personal identifiers.

Members signing in to /my-ahl/

  • Your email address (must be @ahlab.org), name, and Google profile photo, obtained via Google Sign-In and used solely to identify you as a current lab member.
  • Records of which member-only actions you perform on the site (e.g. submitting a publication or press entry).

Members who add a wallet membership card

  • Your name, role, lab affiliation period, and a link to your public lab profile page — packaged into a Google Wallet or Apple Wallet pass.
  • A unique pass identifier so we can update or revoke your pass later.
  • The pass is delivered to your device through Google or Apple, who store and display it on your behalf under their own terms.

We do not collect payment information, location, or any data beyond what is listed above.

3. How we use it

  • To run and improve ahlab.org.
  • To verify that members signing in are genuine lab members.
  • To issue, update, and revoke membership wallet passes.
  • To respond to enquiries you send us.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We do not use it for advertising.

4. Third parties

The site relies on a small number of trusted services:

  • Google Analytics — anonymised usage analytics.
  • Google Sign-In — member authentication.
  • Google Wallet — issuing and storing Android membership passes on your device.
  • Apple Wallet — issuing and storing iOS membership passes on your device.

Each service handles your data under its own privacy terms. We share only the minimum data each service requires.

5. Retention and revocation

  • Analytics and server logs are retained for up to 14 months and then deleted or aggregated.
  • Member records are retained while you are an active or alumni member of the lab.
  • Wallet passes remain on your device until you remove them, or until we revoke them (for example, when a member leaves the lab).
  • You can remove a wallet pass at any time from the Google Wallet or Apple Wallet app on your device.

6. Your rights

You can ask us to:

  • tell you what personal data we hold about you,
  • correct anything that is wrong,
  • delete your member record (where we are not required to retain it),
  • revoke or void your wallet pass.

Contact us at contact@ahlab.org for any of the above. We aim to respond within 14 days.

7. Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of the page. Continued use of the site after a change means you accept the updated policy.
