Nuwan Tharaka Perera

Research Attachment

Tharaka is an electronic and telecommunication undergraduate of University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka. He has developed a passion for electronics during his school age. He is personally greatly interested in robotics, embedded system development, and IoT applications. He enjoys facing challenges like robotics competitions and code challenges.

He likes to spend his leisure time listening to music and observing the night sky.
