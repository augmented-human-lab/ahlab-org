Kian Peen is a Senior Embedded Systems Engineer. He has a MEng from NUS majoring in Acoustics. His interests is in developing electronics hardware in areas of IOT, wireless sensors, wearables and assistive technologies. He is also an entrepreneur, having started his own company since 2014.
