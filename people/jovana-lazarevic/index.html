Jovana Lazarević

Research Attachment

Jovana is a final year student of bachelor studies in biomedical engineering at the Faculty of Technical Sciences (University of Novi Sad, Serbia). She is fascinated by artificial intelligence, digital health and future of storytelling in virtual environments. Driven by curiosity in cognitive psychology she joined forces with our PhD candidate Samantha Chan and together they will elucidate how our eye movements differ when we are exploring the new environment versus environments we are already used to. The results they'll obtain hold a big potential since they can be coupled with emotionally responsive virtual reality setup (another project Jovana is working on) and thus deliver more user-personal oriented experience.

In her free time, Jovana likes to click analogue photos and be a big philosopher about life itself.
