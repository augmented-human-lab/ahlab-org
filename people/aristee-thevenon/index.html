Aristée Thevenon

Research Attachment

Aristée Thevenon is an intern from the French National University of Civil Aviation, where he is a second-year engineer student specialised in embedded systems. During this year, he worked on mutual awareness and tangible interactions for a tactile overhead panel. With this internship, he hopes to improve his knowledge in Human-Computer Interface.

Apart from his studies, he enjoys videography and aeroplane piloting.
