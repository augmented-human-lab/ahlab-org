Chamod grew up in Colombo Sri Lanka. He completed his bachelor's degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka. He joined the Augmented Human Lab at SUTD as a research intern in 2016 and is currently working as a research engineer. In his free time he likes to read or go for long walks.
