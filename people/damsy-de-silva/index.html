Damsy De Silva

Intern

Damsy De Silva is an engineering undergraduate at the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka, specializing in Computer Engineering. She is currently working as a research intern at the Augmented Human Lab. Her interests lie in Computer Vision, Computer Architecture, Embedded Systems, and Wearable Devices.

She is passionate about teaching, and she has worked as a Teaching Assistant at the Department of Computer Engineering, University of Peradeniya. She is also a chess, badminton player and loves to watch movies and TV shows.
