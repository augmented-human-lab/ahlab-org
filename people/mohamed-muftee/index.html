Mohamed Muftee

Intern
Personal Website ↗

Muftee is an intern from Sri Lanka and an undergraduate student at the University of Moratuwa. He is pursuing a Bachelor of Science (Hons) degree in Engineering concentrating in Electronic and Telecommunication Engineering. His primary focus at the lab involves Human Computer Interaction, Emebedded AI, Signal processing and Electronic prototyping, with a broader interest in integrating technology to enhance Human well-being. In his free time, he enjoys playing cricket, exploring cultures and meeting new people.
