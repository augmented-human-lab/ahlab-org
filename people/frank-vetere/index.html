Frank Vetere

Professor

Frank is the director Microsoft Research Centre for Social Natural User Interfaces and lead the Interaction Design Laboratory, both at the University of Melbourne. His expertise is in Human-Computer Interactions (HCI), with particular interests in Social Computing, Natural User Interfaces, and technologies for ageing well. More about Frank: http://people.eng.unimelb.edu.au/fv/
