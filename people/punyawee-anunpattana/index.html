Punyawee Anunpattana

Research Fellow
Personal Website ↗

Punyawee is a Research Fellow at the National University of Singapore (NUS), where he works on AI-driven adaptive learning, gamification, and educational technology. He holds a Ph.D. in Information Science from the Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (JAIST), where his research focused on game informatics, human-computer interaction, and the transformative potential of AI and gamification in education. His work is centered on making learning more engaging and effective, with a particular emphasis on enhancing curiosity in the learning process.

In his leisure time, Punyawee enjoys playing games, watching films and TV series, and exploring new places. He has a passion for experiencing different cultures and landscapes. A true coffee enthusiast, he also enjoys making coffee during his free time.
