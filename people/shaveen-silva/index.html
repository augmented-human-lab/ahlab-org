Shaveen Silva is an undergraduate in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Moratuwa. He is currently interning at the Augmented Human Lab, where he works on AiSee, a smart headset that uses AI to help blind and low-vision people better perceive their surroundings. His interests span Agentic AI and intelligent systems, and he loves diving into deep tech and understanding how things work at a fundamental level. When he is not thinking about AI and interfaces, Shaveen is usually buried in a book, watching movies, catching the latest Formula 1 race or hunting for good sci fi. He also loves to travel, go hiking and generally collect new hobbies faster than he can list them.
