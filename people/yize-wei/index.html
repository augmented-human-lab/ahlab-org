Yize Wei

PhD Candidate
Personal Website ↗

Yize is a 3rd-year PhD Candidate in the Computer Science department at National University of Singapore (NUS), advised by Suranga Nanayakkara and Roger Zimmermann . She also works closely with Christophe Jouffrais and Wei Tsang Ooi . Previously, she received a B.Eng. in Industrial Engineering Management (with a minor in French Language Studies) from NUS in 2022. She has also worked as a software engineer intern at Autodesk and a student research assistant in the broader field of AI/ML. Her research interest is situated in the intersection of Accessibility, Human-Robot Interaction, and Human-AI Interaction. To know more about her, please visit her website.
