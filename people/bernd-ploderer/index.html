Bernd Ploderer

Senior Lecturer

Bernd is a Lecturer in Computer Human Interaction at Queensland University of Technology (QUT) and an Honorary Fellow at the University of Melbourne. His research lies at the intersection of computational and social sciences and contributes to the disciplines of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Computer-Supported Cooperative Work (CSCW).

More about Bernd: http://staff.qut.edu.au/staff/ploderer/
