Yifei Luo

Intern

Yifei is an intern from China, currently pursuing her Master’s degree at RWTH Aachen University in Germany. At AHL, she primarily works on audio engineering, human-computer interaction (HCI), and drones. Her background and research interests include HCI, acoustics, machine learning (ML), and assistive technology.

Before joining AHL, Yifei worked as a student research assistant at the Institute of Hearing Technology and Acoustics, as well as the Institute of Man-Machine Interaction at RWTH Aachen University. Prior to her studies at RWTH, she obtained a Bachelor's degree in Automation from Tsinghua University in China, where she also worked as an engineer at the Institute of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Media Integration.
