Alexandru Dancu

Research Fellow

Alexandru Dancu is interested in new ways of using digital information and augmenting human capabilities.He is driven by the excitement of discovery and creating new user interfaces and technologies.

Currently he is a postdoc at the Augmented Human Lab, SUTD and obtained his PhD from Chalmers University of Technology, Sweden, working on body-centric interfaces. He gained his MSc. in Embedded Computing System with the thesis project at University of Southampton, UK. His BSc. degree was in Computer-Science with the thesis project at School of Computing, National University of Singapore.
