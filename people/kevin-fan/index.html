Kevin Fan

Research Attachment

Kevin graduated from the University of British Columbia with a Bachelor’s Degree in Software Engineering. Now Kevin is pursuing PhD in the School of Media Design in Keio University(KMD). Kevin’s research is in the field of HCI, cross-modal sensations, VR, AR. Kevin joined the Augmented Senses group as an intern student.

In leisure time, Kevin enjoys sports such as basketball and cycling, cooking, and eating.

Website: www.kevinfan.com
