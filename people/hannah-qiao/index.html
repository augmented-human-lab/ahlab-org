Hannah Qiao

Designer

Hannah is a designer with a passion for teaching and learning, especially when it comes to working with children. As an artist and graphic designer, she also enjoys working in visual design and incorporating these skills into her work. In her spare time, she likes to read, bake, take photos, and create art.

Hannah has a Master's degree in User Experience Design and a Bachelor's degree in Arts in Education and Psychology.
