Mengmeng Sun

Research Attachment

Mengmeng is a third-year master student from China. She is studying at the Cyber-Physical Interaction Lab, Northwestern Polytechnical University. She comes to the University of Auckland as a visiting research student and will stay in Augmented Human Lab for the next 3 months. Her research interest is about haptic interaction in Augmented Reality. Her master thesis is about the research on the manipulation technology based on augmented haptic in the virtual-physical assembly environment.
