Ryo Hajika

Research Engineer
Personal Website ↗

Ryo is an HCI (Human-Computer Interaction) designer, a software and electronics prototype engineer, and a videographer. He is interested in interaction technologies and new media art. He was previously a research lab technical manager at the School of Built Environment, Massey University (NZ) and a research assistant at the Empathic Computing Laboratory, the University of Auckland (NZ).
