Yvonne Chua

Designer

Yvonne has a ME (Engineering) from University of Auckland, BA (Industrial Design) from the National University of Singapore and believes in creating products that have a tangible impact on users' lives. Before joining AHLab she worked as a product and UX designer in Tware, a haptic wearable tech startup making smart pressure jackets for individuals with sensory processing disorders.

Her Masters research is on immersive technology for learning - developing guidelines for the design of educational virtual reality content for children.
