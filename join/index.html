Open Opportunities

Ways to join our lab

Whether you're an undergraduate, graduate student, or an experienced researcher — there's a place for you at AHLab.

01

NUS Undergraduates

We are open to curious, hardworking students who are not over-committed with other work. Please look at our projects to find out more about what we do, and read about Assistive Augmentation.

Apply Now
02

Graduate Students

As a PhD student, you will be working in the broader area of Assistive Augmentation. Please make sure that your Statement of Purpose is aligned with this vision. Best to reach out via our application form prior to formally applying through NUS Computer Science.

Apply Now
03

Full-Time Positions

We may have full-time positions (Research Fellow, Research Assistant) coming up. We are particularly keen to hire those interested in Assistive Tech, Cognitive Augmentation, Human-Centered AI, Interaction Design, and Wearable & Ubiquitous Computing.

Apply Now
04

Visiting Students

We have limited openings for visiting researchers and interns (undergraduate or graduate students). These are typically self-funded positions.

Apply Now
05

Industry Partners

Augmented Human Lab has had very successful collaborations with industry. We welcome new industry collaborations. If interested, please reach out directly.

What We Value

What we look for

01

Curiosity & Initiative

We value self-starters who ask "what if?" and aren't afraid to explore unconventional ideas across disciplines.

02

Interdisciplinary Thinking

Our best work happens at the intersection of engineering, design, and human sciences. We seek people who bridge domains.

03

Human-Centered Mindset

Technology should serve people. We look for team members who deeply care about the humans their work will impact.
